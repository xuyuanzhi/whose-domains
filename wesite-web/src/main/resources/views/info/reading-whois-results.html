Reading WHOIS Results

How to Interpret WHOIS Data

WHOIS results contain a wealth of information about a domain. Understanding how to read and interpret this data is essential for effective domain research.

Key Sections of WHOIS Results

1. Domain Information

  • Domain Name: The registered domain name
  • Registry Domain ID: Unique identifier assigned by the registry
  • Registrar WHOIS Server: The WHOIS server for the registrar
  • Registrar URL: Website of the domain registrar

2. Important Dates

  • Creation Date: When the domain was first registered
  • Updated Date: Last time the WHOIS record was modified
  • Registry Expiry Date: When the domain registration expires

Tip: Older domains (based on creation date) are often more valuable and trustworthy.

3. Registrar Information

  • Registrar: Company through which the domain was registered
  • Registrar IANA ID: Official identification number of the registrar
  • Registrar Abuse Contact: Email and phone for reporting abuse

4. Domain Status

Status codes indicate the current state and restrictions on the domain:

  • ok: Normal, active domain
  • clientTransferProhibited: Cannot be transferred (common security measure)
  • serverTransferProhibited: Registry-level transfer lock
  • clientDeleteProhibited: Cannot be deleted
  • redemptionPeriod: Expired but can still be renewed
  • pendingDelete: Will be deleted soon

5. Nameservers

Nameservers indicate where the domain's DNS is hosted:

  • Shows the hosting provider or DNS service
  • Can reveal related domains using the same nameservers
  • Useful for identifying the technology stack

6. Contact Information

Three types of contacts may be listed:

  • Registrant: The domain owner
  • Administrative: Person managing administrative tasks
  • Technical: Person handling technical issues

Note: Due to GDPR and privacy protection, this information may be redacted or show privacy service details.

Sample WHOIS Output Explained

Domain Name: EXAMPLE.COM Registry Domain ID: 2336799_DOMAIN_COM-VRSN Registrar WHOIS Server: whois.registrar.com Registrar URL: http://www.registrar.com Updated Date: 2024-01-15T10:30:00Z Creation Date: 1995-08-14T04:00:00Z Registry Expiry Date: 2025-08-13T04:00:00Z Registrar: Example Registrar, Inc. Registrar IANA ID: 1234 Domain Status: clientTransferProhibited Name Server: NS1.EXAMPLE.COM Name Server: NS2.EXAMPLE.COM

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Recently Created: Very new domains may be less trustworthy
  • Frequent Owner Changes: Multiple ownership changes in short time
  • Privacy on Established Domains: Long-time domains suddenly enabling privacy
  • Inconsistent Information: Mismatched contact details
  • Suspicious Nameservers: Known bulletproof hosting

Using WHOIS Data for Research

  • Domain Age: Older domains typically have more authority
  • Owner Patterns: Identify portfolios of domains owned by same entity
  • Expiration Opportunities: Find domains expiring soon for potential acquisition
  • Hosting History: Track changes in nameservers over time
  • Geographic Clues: Contact information may reveal location

Limitations to Keep in Mind

  • Information may not be 100% accurate (relies on registrant honesty)
  • Privacy protection hides true owner details
  • Data may be cached and not reflect recent changes
  • Different TLDs provide different levels of information
  • GDPR has reduced publicly available personal information
